Poultry farming is the raising of birds domestically or commercially, primarily for meat and eggs. Chickens, turkeys, ducks, geese, guinea fowl and squabs are some of the examples of poultry farming which are of primary importance to humans.

PIG FARMING

Pig farming is the raising and breeding of domestic pigs as livestock which is a branch of animal husbandry. Pigs are farmed principally for food and skins.
